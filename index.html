Welcome to Beckett Beans

Welcome to Beckett Beans, where every cup tells a story. We are a small, independent coffee shop committed to serving the finest coffee in town.

Whether you're looking for a quick espresso to start your day or a cozy spot to relax with friends, we've got you covered.

What We Offer

Premium Coffee

We source our beans from sustainable farms around the world and roast them locally to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

Fresh Pastries

Our bakery items are made fresh daily by local bakers. From croissants to muffins, there's something for everyone.

Cozy Atmosphere

With comfortable seating and free Wi-Fi, our shop is the perfect place to work, study, or catch up with friends.
