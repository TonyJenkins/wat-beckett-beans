Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer Wi-Fi?

Yes! We offer free Wi-Fi to all customers. The password is available at the counter.

Do you have vegan options?

Absolutely! We have almond, oat, and soy milk alternatives, as well as several vegan pastries and sandwiches.

Can I reserve a table?

We operate on a first-come, first-served basis. However, we do accept reservations for groups of 6 or more.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering for events, meetings, and special occasions. Please contact us for more information.
